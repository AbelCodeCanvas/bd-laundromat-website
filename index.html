About Our Company

Serving Johannesburg with pride and professionalism since 2018

Our Story

BD Laundromat & Logistic was founded by Blessed Dube with a vision to provide reliable, affordable laundry services combined with efficient logistics solutions for the Johannesburg community.

What started as a small laundromat in Kew has grown into a comprehensive service provider trusted by hundreds of families and businesses across Gauteng.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional laundry and logistics services that save our customers time and deliver outstanding value.

Our Vision

To be Johannesburg's preferred choice for laundry and logistics services through innovation and customer satisfaction.
